Description

Tasiyan is a visual manifestation of the contradictions and tensions deeply embedded within culture and society, brought to life in a tangible and poignant form to reveal how these cultural knots can act as heavy barriers to growth and creativity.

About this collection

This collection draws inspiration from the hidden wounds that permeate the core of culture and society, surfacing as "cultural abscesses." *Tasiyan* seeks to depict the traditions and beliefs that, rather than empowering individuals, entrap them within limiting confines, replacing freedom with fear and restriction. These works echo the struggles of those left powerless under cultural pressures, trapped amidst an overwhelming web of norms and taboos, with their resistance expressed only through these artistic creations.

The collection raises a fundamental question: how do outdated cultural norms and beliefs transform into psychological and societal burdens that not only restrain individuals but also root themselves in the fabric of society, hindering its progress?
